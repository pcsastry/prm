About PMS

This project "PRISON MANAGEMENT SYSTEM" is a collection of registers and reports for the effective management of prisons. This system contains the modules like nominal roll, case register, parole register, Interview requests, In-out register and an automated release diary generator.


The details of the prisoner and his/her demographic details are captured. A digital photo comprising different views of the prisoner and the list of articles surrendered by prisoner during nominal roll are recorded.


All the details of the cases against the prisoners are captured. This includes the sentence details, remand/conviction details, etc.This report will display the list of prisoners to be released on a day, the next day, the next week, the next month, or any given duration of time. The system considers the reduction of sentence length due to various considerations.


This module tracks all prisoners on parole and provides necessary reports on this data. All interview requests by the relatives of the prisoners are recorded and tracked. An in-out register will track all prisoners and others who move in and out for various reasons. This includes provisions for recording the prisoners sent to courts for hearing. Various status reports and demographical analysis reports are generated
